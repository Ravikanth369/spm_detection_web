Why is AI-Powered Spam Detection Useful?

1. Hidden Spam & Phishing Attacks: Some emails appear genuine but contain malicious links or scams.

2. Large-Scale Email Filtering: Businesses and email providers need automated spam filtering.

3. Real-Time Protection: AI prevents spam emails from reaching inboxes before they cause harm.

4. Advanced Pattern Recognition: The model detects spam based on text, keywords, and phishing links.
